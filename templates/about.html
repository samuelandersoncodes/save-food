About Save Food

The idea erupted from a friend's PhD project. While He explained his project ideas to me, I felt the need to contribute my quota to solving the problem in question. It came to realization that 30% of food which is estimated at 2.5 billion tons and worth $230 billion is wasted annualy in recent multi-dimensional surveys.

Global food waste has an enormous ill environmental and human impact. Food waste is a huge source of greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions and wasted natural resources. Therefore reducing food waste could help to reduce global greenhouse gas emissions, establish food security and encourage healthy food systems as well as feed those in need of food.

I therefore thought it wise to pilot this project in order to bridge the river of food surplus and the mouths that could make good use of them. I have high hopes that this pilot project will grow to its fullest and help save lives and the planet.
